FAQ

Common
inquiries

What AI models does Armory support?

Armory integrates natively with GPT-4 Turbo, Claude 3 Opus, Gemini Ultra, and Perplexity. You can also connect any OpenAI-compatible endpoint, including locally hosted models via Ollama or vLLM.

How does the annual billing discount work?

Annual plans are billed upfront at a flat 20% discount compared to the monthly rate. You can switch between billing cycles at any time — unused credit is prorated automatically.

Is Armory SOC 2 compliant?

Yes. Enterprise tier customers receive full SOC 2 Type II compliance documentation, GDPR data processing agreements, and on-premise deployment options for sensitive data environments.

Can I migrate from another automation platform?

Absolutely. We provide migration tooling for n8n, Zapier, and Make workflows. Our engineering team offers white-glove migration support for Enterprise tier customers at no additional cost.

What happens if I exceed my API call limit?

You'll receive a notification at 80% usage. Exceeding the limit won't cause downtime — overage is billed at a per-call rate with a hard cap option available to prevent unexpected charges.
